Options

The S.P.P. book-ediiton mentions gives 2 listings (general & specific) that could prove useful:

  • 4 general
    • 📖 congregations
    • 📖 free clinics of schools for counseling, physiology, psychiatry, etc.
    • 📖 community-health centers
    • 📖 government hospitals
  • 9 specific
    • 📖 Suicide Prevention Hotline (now re-named to the "988 Lifeline" mid-2022) 🇺🇸
    • 📖 FindTreatment.gov of the Substance Abuse & Mental Health Services Administration (S.A.M.H.S.A.)'s Center for Behavioral Health Statistics & Quality (C.B.H.S.Q.) 🇺🇸
    • 📖 National Alliance on Mental Illness (N.A.M.I.)
    • 📖 Mental Health Alliance (now "Mental Health America, Inc.")
    • 📖 National Parent & Youth Helpline™ (of Parents Anonymous® Inc.)
    • 📖 Alcoholics Anonymous® (of Alcoholics Anonymous World Services, Inc.)
    • 📖 Narcotics Anonymous (N.A.®) (of NA World Services, Inc.)
    • 📖 National Association of Community Health Centers, Inc. (N.A.C.H.C.)
    • 📖 TEEN Challenge (now "Adult & Teen Challenge"; Christian/addiction treatment)
